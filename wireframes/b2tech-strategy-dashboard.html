Strategic Bets & Market Whitespace
Bet 01
Hybrid Risk Scoring
Moving from static rule-only detection to a layered scoring system combining configurable rules with behavioral and device signals. The central Risk product investment for FY2026.
Competitive Moat Against
BtoBet · Digitain — both weakest on AI/Automation
⚠ Key Risk
ML quality requires labelled data — sparse in early African deployments. Mitigate: rules-weighted launch, ML weight increases as data matures.
Bet 02
Africa-Native Identity
Direct integrations with African mobile money providers and local identity brokers as KYC signal layer. SIM metadata + mobile money account status — low-friction, locally trusted.
Competitive Moat Against
All 5 competitors — Pronet leads but coverage remains limited
⚠ Key Risk
Regulatory acceptance varies by country. Mitigate: position as supplementary signal, not standalone; document per-market acceptance before launch.
Bet 03
Omnichannel Risk Enforcement
All risk decisions — fraud flags, self-exclusion, withdrawal holds — must propagate instantly across digital, USSD, and retail POS. Non-negotiable for African operators.
Competitive Moat Against
Digitain · Pronet — retail integration still maturing for both
⚠ Key Risk
Retail POS integration depth varies across operator base. Mitigate: channel audit before go-live; compliance checklist + integration support provided.
